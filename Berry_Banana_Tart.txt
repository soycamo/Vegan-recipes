Berry Banana Tart

  Crust
  - 2 or 3 red bananas cut in pieces
  - Flour?
  - Cocoa powder?

Blend bananas with a blender or food processor with flour and cocoa powder. Form
into a crust in a bowl. Bake in oven for 10-20 min. 

  Filling
    - 1 c blueberries 
    - red wine 

Boil blueberries with red wine. Pour into crust and bake, covering with a crumbly
topping.
